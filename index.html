Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Get answers to the most commonly asked questions about services

How to make an order?

For food & beverages

STEP 01:

Click this link to go to our webapp & select order for food

STEP 02:

Search or select a restaurant to order food or beverages from.

STEP 03:

A menu will pop up with a list of meals available for order. select your desired dishes and add them to cart

STEP 04:

We dispatch our delivery personnel to deliver your meal within 15 mins - 30mins, while it's still hot!!!

For Liquor and Chasers

STEP 01:

Click this link to go to our webapp & select order for alcoholic drinks

STEP 02:

A page will open up with a list of alcoholic drinks available. Simply search and select your desired drinks.

STEP 03:

You will be prompted to give delivery details and choose a payment method.

STEP 04:

We dispatch our delivery personnel to deliver your drinks within 15 mins - 30mins

How long do you take to make a delivery?

Our deliveries take an average of 15 mins - 30 mins. Incase of any unforseen delays, we will be prompt to notify you

How much do you charge for deliveries?

Deliveries are charged based on the distance covered. Our minimum charge per delivery is Ksh.60 for every 5km.

What payment options do you accept?

We currently only accept two payment options:

  • Cash on delivery
  • Mpesa

We will soon start accepting Debit & Credit cards and Paypal payment options.
